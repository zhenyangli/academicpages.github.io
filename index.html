Who am I?

I'm Zhenyang Li! Currently I'm working as Postdoctoral Researcher @ QUVA Deep Vision Lab, University of Amsterdam.

I was a Ph.D. student in Computer Vision @ University of Amsterdam, where I closely collaborated with Prof. Cees Snoek, and Prof. Arnold Smeulders. My research lie on computer vision and machine learning in general. And I mainly work on object classification and localization, action recognition in videos.
